About Us

Since the early 1900s, TheresaShoes has been crafting shoes by hand in the heart of our country's shoemaking capital, Marikina City. Across each generation, our family has carried over a tradition rooted in quality leather, thread, and craftmanship into shoes our customers can rely on. Every pair carries the care of three generations, and the commitment to give you footwear you'll feel confident in every step of the way.
